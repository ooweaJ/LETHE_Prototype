## Weapon rhythm / echo transformation design correction

- Reviewed Claude's document changes around no-air-swing weapon targeting and weapon-flavored echoes.
- Accepted the core direction:
  - no empty melee swings.
  - twin blades auto-target nearest enemy.
  - greatsword targets the densest arc.
  - echoes and ultimates are implemented once, then flavored by weapon rhythm.
- Corrected the data model from a single `echoScale` to split parameters:
  - `echoSizeScale`
  - `echoDamageScale`
  - `echoProcStyle`
  - `ultimatePattern`
- Locked the Blood Blade Storm weapon expression:
  - twin blades: many small blood blades, fast rotation, repeated chained cuts.
  - greatsword: fewer large blood blades, slow rotation or 2-3 huge slashes, strong hitstop and bigger screen shake.
- Updated:
  - `docs/design/LETHE_DESIGN_02_COMBAT.md`

- Implemented the first code pass for the weapon rhythm rule.

  - do not swing if no enemy is inside `TwinBladeRange * 1.15`.
