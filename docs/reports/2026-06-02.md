## Decisions made

- Permission prompts still pause Codex work and must be approved in the Codex UI.
- Discord notices are used as attention/status signals, not as remote approval buttons.
- Work should be checkpointed before stopping when the next resume step may be ambiguous.
- GPT and Claude prompts do not need to be sent to both reviewers by default.
